    Welcome to Our Creative Community

    Join us in our mission to combine creativity and environmental sustainability. We connect customers with unique, handmade products while providing opportunities for artists to transform discarded materials into inspiring, innovative creations.

    Target Audience

    We are looking for talented individuals who are passionate about:

    • Handmade crafts
    • Recycling and upcycling unused materials
    • Environmental sustainability
    • Inspiring others through creativity

    We accept students and graduates from:

    • Faculty of Specific Education of Art
    • Faculty of Education, Department of Arts
    • Faculty of Arts
    • Faculty of Applied Arts

    Why Join Us?

    By joining our team, you will:

    • Be part of a growing community of eco-conscious artists
    • Have a platform to showcase and sell your creations
    • Contribute to making a positive impact on the environment
    • Inspire others with your unique designs
